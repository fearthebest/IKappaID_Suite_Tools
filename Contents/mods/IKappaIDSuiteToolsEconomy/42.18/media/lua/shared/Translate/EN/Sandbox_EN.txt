Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy = IKappaID Suite Tools — Economy
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyEnabled = Turn on the money system (requires PhoneShop mod)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyWireDistance = How far away you can send money to someone
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyShopDistance = How close you must be to use a shop
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyShopTilesOnly = Shops only work on IKST economy tiles (not any container)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyShopProtect = Stop players from stealing items out of shop stock
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyShopCapacity = How much a shop can hold (Max 100, keeps food cold)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomySalesTax = Tax percentage on shop sales
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyTaxReceiver = Who gets the tax money (put their username here)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyAtmRequired = Must use an ATM to put in or take out money
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyValuables = Let players sell expensive items for cash
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyMaxVendPrice = Highest price allowed for one item in a shop
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyCurrencyName = Name of the money (like Dollars, Credits, etc.)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyZombieBounty = Give money to players for killing zombies
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyZombieBountyChance = Chance to get paid for each kill (0 to 100)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyZombieBountyMin = Lowest amount of money for a kill
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyZombieBountyMax = Highest amount of money for a kill
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyIdCardBanking = Virtual bank accounts with Base.IDcard access (no physical cash; Steam ID accounts)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyIdCardPlayerReissue = Let players replace a lost bank ID at an ATM (fee and cooldown apply)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyIdCardReissueFee = Bank balance fee to replace a lost bank ID at an ATM
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyIdCardReissueCooldownHours = Hours between player-initiated bank ID replacements (0 = no cooldown)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyZombieBountyToBank = Put kill money in the bank instead of giving cash (ignored when ID card banking is on)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyMinWireAmount = Smallest amount you can wire to someone
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyWireFeePercent = Fee taken from wired money (0 to 50 percent)
Sandbox_IKappaIDSuiteToolsEconomy_EconomyShopOwnerStockOnly = Only the shop owner can put items in the shop
