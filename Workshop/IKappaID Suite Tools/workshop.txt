description=Build 42 admin suite: claims, recovery journal, server utilities, and optional World Edit / Vehicles / Economy addons. [b]Testing phase[/b] — help welcome on private and small servers.
description=
description=[b]Testing — your reports help[/b]
description=IKST is in active testing toward a stable 1.0 release. If anything errors, dupes, denies wrongly, or feels off, please send a report with logs. More real-server tests = faster fixes for everyone.
description=
description=[b]What to send[/b]
description=1. [b]DebugLog-server.txt[/b] (dedicated MP) or client DebugLog (SP / listen host) from the session
description=2. IKST version (e.g. 0.2.6) and Build 42 version (e.g. 42.19)
description=3. Dedicated server or listen host?
description=4. What you did, expected vs actual
description=5. Other mods enabled (or ModManager list) — especially economy, vehicles, safehouse, and UI mods
description=
description=[b]Report via[/b] Steam discussion, GitHub Issues, Ko-fi, or Discord [b]callmekappaid[/b] — logs beat vague “it broke.”
description=
description=A clear control panel for multiplayer servers and the people who play on them — land and vehicle claims, recovery journal, and optional world tools. Matches the dark layout and orange highlights of IKappaID Phone Shop.
description=
description=[b]What you get (base mod)[/b]
description=- Open the panel anywhere with [b]Ctrl+Shift+W[/b]
description=- Claim safehouses and vehicles; add members; choose who may enter, drive, or use storage
description=- Recovery journal: save your character on an item and restore skills and progress after death
description=- Server management: player care, weather and time, moderation, backups, and everyday utilities
description=- Plain-English sandbox options with categories (Claims, Admin, Security, and more) for claim limits, guest access, and journal settings
description=
description=[b]Optional addons[/b] — turn on only what your server needs
description=- [b]World Edit[/b] — paint and remove tiles, inspect builds, protect areas, copy blueprints, password locks for containers
description=- [b]Vehicles[/b] — list, move, repair, and remove vehicles (for hosts and trusted staff)
description=- [b]Economy[/b] — bank accounts, transfers, and player-run shops (works with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3749926419]IKappaID Phone Shop[/url])
description=- [b]Loot[/b] — repopulate container loot with vanilla room distributions (admin)
description=
description=[b]For players[/b]
description=Register your home and vehicles from the Claim workspace, or right-click a car for quick options. What you can do depends on what the server allows — many servers let you manage your own claims without needing elevated access.
description=
description=[b]For server hosts[/b]
description=Claims and permissions are handled on the server so everyone sees the same rules. Full tools are available to admins; player self-service is optional and controlled in sandbox options.
description=
